Class Management Platform

by five of SteveJobs Academy's students,

This product is a management software with the intent of automate all of the usual operations used by the Steve Jobs Academy regarding the managing of all the students, the teachers, the subjects and the grades.

Awesome UX

Thanks to a minimal and intuitive interface, the platform offers an astonishing experience, suitable for every type of user.

Open Source

The product is under GNU General Public License v3.0, every developer can make a contribution or use the platform totally free of charge.

BETA Release

If the BETA version doesn't suck, it means it has been launched too late. In our case, we outdid ourselves!

Swagger Documentation

The server-side API'S provided by our platform are documentated and easily testable thanks to the tools offered by Swagger.
